The Three Plays

```
01
Green Light Use
⏱ 7 minutes
Study Guide Generator
Claude.ai · NotebookLM

Paste your syllabus or lecture notes — watch AI organize, compress, and turn raw content into a structured study guide with practice questions in under 30 seconds.

Setup & Framing
90 sec

Open Claude.ai on your laptop. Project the screen. No setup required — this is the simplest demo.

🎤

"Every one of you is managing four to six courses at once, maybe a job, an org, a family. You don't have time to re-read 80 pages before a midterm. This tool doesn't replace studying — it compresses the path to it."

Live Demo — Paste & Generate
2 min

Paste a syllabus section or lecture notes excerpt into Claude. Use this prompt:

Prompt — Study Guide 
You are a study coach for a college student preparing for a midterm exam.
```

Based on the notes I’ll paste below, create a study guide that includes:

1. The 5 most important concepts (2-sentence explanation each)
1. 10 practice questions with answers
1. A 3-bullet “the night before” review checklist

Here are my notes:
[PASTE NOTES HERE]
```
Audience Try-Time
3 min
  • Have scholars open Claude.ai on their phones
  • Tell them: paste notes from any class they're in right now
  • Walk the room — look at what's being generated
  • Call on one scholar to share what they got
⚠️

Ethics moment: "Notice the prompt says 'based on my notes' — you fed it YOUR material. That's green light use. You're using AI to organize your thinking, not bypass it. Yellow light would be asking it to write your paper. Red light would be submitting AI output as your own analysis on a graded exam."

Close & Hand Off
90 sec
🎤

"This took you 30 seconds. Your TA takes 30 minutes. Neither replaced your brain — but one of them is available at 2am before your exam. Demo two is harder."

02
Voice + Text Hybrid
⏱ 8 minutes
Voice Interview Coach
Claude.ai Mobile · Voice Mode · Mellow Voice

A live AI behavioral interview using Claude's voice mode. Volunteer speaks, Claude listens and asks STAR method questions out loud. The room watches NLP happen in real time.

⚡ AV DEPENDENCY: Claude's voice must route through house speakers. Test Bluetooth pairing to mixer at 12:45 PM. See AV Runbook for full audio setup.
Pre-Demo Setup (before 1:15 PM)
Before session
  • Claude app open on iPhone 17 Pro Max — Claude Max logged in
  • Voice mode tested — select Mellow voice
  • Phone audio verified through house speakers
  • Backup typed prompt ready in Notes app
  • Do Not Disturb ON · Auto-lock set to Never
Phase 1 — Setup Prompt (typed)
30 sec

Type this into Claude before activating voice. Ask volunteer for their target role/company type first.

Setup Prompt — Type This First 
You are conducting a behavioral interview for a [ROLE] 
```

internship at a [COMPANY TYPE].

Interview me using the STAR method. Ask one behavioral
question at a time. After I answer, give brief verbal
feedback on what was strong and what to sharpen.

Be direct and professional. Start with your first question.
```
Phase 2 — Activate Voice Mode
30 sec
  • Tap the soundwave icon in Claude app
  • Briefly show the five voice options to the room
  • Select Mellow — clearest for group settings
  • Let Claude speak the first behavioral question out loud
🎤

"What you're hearing is Claude speaking to us. That's text-to-speech powered by ElevenLabs, running on top of the same model that just read our setup prompt. Watch what it does when [volunteer] answers."

Phase 3 — Volunteer Interview
3–4 min
  • Volunteer speaks their answer — they hold the wireless mic + face the phone
  • Narrate as transcript appears: "Watch the transcript — speech recognition in real time"
  • Claude gives verbal feedback — room hears it through speakers
  • Run second question the same way
  • HARD STOP at 2 questions
📍 Phone positioning: Place phone on stand 2–3 feet from volunteer, mic side facing them. The volunteer speaks toward phone (Claude hears) AND into wireless mic (room hears). Test this during AV setup.
Phase 4 — Switch to Text
90 sec

Tap Stop on voice mode. Type this prompt. Project results on screen.

Text Critique Prompt 
Based on the two answers, give me:
```

1. Strongest thing about my interview style
1. ONE thing to fix before my real interview
1. A better version of my second answer, written out
🎤

“Voice was the experience. Text is the artifact. Screenshot that critique. Study it tonight. The voice version is memorable — the text version is usable.”

```

30 sec
🎤

"What you just watched is NLP — natural language processing — made real. Three things happened: One, speech recognition converted spoken English to text tokens. Two, language understanding analyzed those tokens against thousands of successful behavioral interview answers. Three, generation produced a response calibrated to what [volunteer] actually said. That full pipeline in under 4 seconds. That is why this technology is changing every interview, every job, every field you're walking into."
```

```
12:45 PM — Most Critical Step

Audio Routing

Work through these options in order. Stop when one works.

A — Bluetooth to Mixer
Settings → Bluetooth → pair to mixer. Have AV tech confirm Claude's voice through house speakers. Adjust input gain.
B — HDMI Audio Extraction
Ask AV tech to route HDMI audio from phone to house speakers via matrix switcher. Already connected from screen mirror step.
C — Portable BT Speaker
Pair JBL Flip 7 to iPhone via Bluetooth. Place at front of room. JBL Flip 7 at full volume reaches ~40ft clearly.
D — Phone Speaker + Mic
Last resort. Place phone face-up, hold wireless mic 6" above speaker. Works but adds echo. Trigger backup plan instead.
12:55 PM
```
